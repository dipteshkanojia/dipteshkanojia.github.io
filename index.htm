Biography: I am a Research Scholar at the IITB-Monash Research Academy also associated with the Center for Indian Languages Technology (CFILT). Formerly, a Research Engineer with CFILT, I joined Ph.D. at the academy in January, 2016. My research interests are Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, and broadly Artificial Intelligence. ;)

Also, I am a Computer Science & Engineering graduate from Uttar Pradesh Technical University (2009-2013).
